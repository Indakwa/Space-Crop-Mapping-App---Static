    • Mapping of crops is important to various stakeholders; Researchers need such information to estimate the impact of climate change on agriculture, calculate yield gaps (difference between actual and potential yields), or analyze the historical evolution of farming systems. Policymakers, donors, and investors need it to better target agricultural and rural development investments and policies. In this project we propose to map a wheat farm by using the combination of optical vegetation indices and synthetic aperture radar (SAR) data. The Sentinel satellites imagery will used to identify Wheat farm with a high temporal and spatial resolution.
